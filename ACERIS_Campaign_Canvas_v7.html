The MBTI Analogy — Use This in Campaign Content
People understand personality tests. They know what it means for a test to read who they are and produce something specific to that result. The MBTI analogy makes the diagnostic-to-platform journey immediately legible — without requiring any explanation of how AI works. Use this in Phase 2 and Phase 3 content.
The Test
The AI Readiness Diagnostic — 30 minutes, structured questions. Maps your business type, stage, and top priorities.
The Result
Your business DNA profile — which modules activate, what configuration the platform builds for you, and what price reflects your needs.
The Product
The SLC platform — already fitted to your business before you log in. No manual setup. No onboarding. It already knows you.
